About Me

I'm a Mechanical Aerospace Engineer with 4+ years of specialized experience in aerospace structures, composite materials, and additive manufacturing. My expertise spans advanced FEA simulations, structural analysis, and innovation-driven engineering solutions.

My passion lies in optimizing structural integrity, developing innovative manufacturing processes, and mentoring engineering teams to achieve excellence in complex aerospace challenges.
